About EchoBox

Open source audio player for everyone

EchoBox is a modern audio player designed with simplicity and functionality in mind. Built with Python and Tkinter, it offers a clean interface with powerful features for managing and playing your music collection.

Whether you're a casual listener or a music enthusiast, EchoBox provides everything you need - from basic playback controls to advanced playlist management and instant search.
